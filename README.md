# 🌍 CarbonIQ – AI-Powered Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform

CarbonIQ helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their environmental impact through AI-powered sustainability insights, carbon footprint analytics, smart recommendations, and interactive what-if simulations.

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Many people want to live sustainably but lack visibility into how their daily activities contribute to carbon emissions. CarbonIQ bridges this gap by transforming everyday actions into measurable environmental impact and providing personalized guidance for reducing footprints.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🤖 AI Climate Coach
- Personalized sustainability guidance
- Carbon reduction recommendations
- Natural language interaction

### 📊 Carbon Analytics Dashboard
- Real-time footprint tracking
- Category-wise emission breakdown
- Historical trend analysis

### 🔮 Carbon Forecasting
- Predict future emissions
- Identify consumption patterns
- Long-term sustainability planning

### ⚡ What-If Simulator
- Simulate lifestyle changes
- Compare environmental impact
- Instant carbon savings calculation

### 🏆 Sustainability Leaderboard
- Gamified environmental engagement
- Compare progress with others
- Encourage eco-friendly habits

### 📄 Smart Receipt Analysis
- Extract purchases from receipts
- Estimate associated carbon emissions
- Automatic categorization
